// 03 · OUR PROCESS

Quality, supervised
at every stage.

We don't just inspect the finished product — our in-house QA team verifies every input, every cure cycle and every dispatch. That's how we keep IS 458 compliance, every single time.

01
Raw Material Inspection

Cement, aggregate and steel are tested at intake. Only batches that meet IS specifications enter our yard.

02
Reinforcement & Mould Setup

Steel cages are fabricated to NP2/NP3 spec. Moulds are calibrated for dimensional accuracy before every pour.

03
Controlled Casting & Curing

Mix design, vibration, demoulding and curing cycles are monitored to lock in strength and surface finish.

04
Final QA & Despatch

Every product is inspected, marked and loaded with care. We deliver across Maharashtra with our trusted logistics partners.
